About us

Istanbul Aydin University Information Center was established in 2003 to support the education and research activities of our university, to provide our students with a more comfortable working and research area, and to meet their information and document needs. In 2015, the P Block library began providing services on the Florya campus, and in 2020, the T Block library began providing services.

It serves 3500 people at the same time in a total closed area of ​​10500 m².

Istanbul Aydin University Information Center has adopted the modern library approach since its establishment. All publications coming to our library are transferred to the KOHA automation program and the collective catalogue created on our web page is available to researchers 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. It is updated with current information sources to meet the information needs of researchers.

Along with new trends in library automation and information access resources, the developments in hardware and software have been closely monitored to ensure that existing resources reach the reader in the best way possible, and an infrastructure has been created in electronic librarianship techniques. In this way, it aims to meet the need for information with the latest technologies in terms of hardware and design, and at the same time to transfer all documents in printed and digital media to the user in the latest and most accurate way.

Istanbul Aydin University Information Center's new and up-to-date collections include collections such as literature and history research collection and medical books collection for medical students. There are also periodicals and reference sources. In addition, computers are also provided to our users in order to support academic research. Istanbul Aydin University Information Center, which provides students with a comfortable research and study environment with private study rooms, single study areas, computer areas and a drawing class, is open for use 24/7.
