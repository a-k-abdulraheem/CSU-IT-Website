General rules

  • The information center is under uninterrupted control 24/7 by security cameras, Electronic Control System (RFID) and security guards for the safety of users and the collection.
  • In cases where the security gate at the entrance gives a warning signal, users may be searched and their bags may be checked by the security guard.
  • Mobile phones must be put on silent mode and no calls should be made within the information center.
  • Consumption of food and beverages other than water is prohibited outside of certain areas.
  • You are responsible for your personal belongings while using the information center. Library or security personnel cannot be held responsible for any theft or loss that may occur.
  • Working in groups or out loud is prohibited within the information centre.
  • Group room reservations are made for groups of at least 4 people. Room usage time is 2 hours.
  • Library security may check ID cards at any time
  • Users can send their wishes and requests by e-mail to or convey them verbally to the Information Center staff
  • From the moment they enter the Information Center, users must comply with the Information Center usage rules. Users who do not comply with these rules will be penalized according to the "Istanbul Aydin University Disciplinary Directive"
